Computer Science Engineering


About this course

Computer Science engineering (CSE) deals with design, implementation, and management of information system of both software & hardware processes. A computer scientist specializes in the theory of computation and design of computational systems.

Pursuing Computer Science & Engineering from the best CSE engineering college in Punjab gives you an extra edge. It caters with thriving research environment, advanced industry sponsored infrastructure and stimulating teaching aid. Our program covers all fundamental as well as advanced concepts of engineering and computing, paired with a chance to gain exposure in emerging technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), Mobile Computing, and Open Source and Open Standard.


Scope

According to US Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment for computer engineers is projected to grow 17 percent from 2014 to 2024.

Computer Science and Engineering @ Rockstar University is highly recognised as one of the premier Engineering programs in North India. It is the first choice for best minds across the region as top companies such as Amazon, Cisco, Google, Infosys, EY, SAP, Wipro, HP hire our Computer Science Engineering graduates.

We have strong academic collaborations with several related industries of repute such as nVidia, Microsoft, SAP, Quick Heal Academy, Infosys, ARM, NXP, Oracle, CISCO, Ericsson, RASCO and many more to make sure that our students are industry ready right from day one.

Our program covers all fundamentals, working and expert subjects that provide a holistic learning environment. The students get apprised to a solid background on engineering fundamentals that allow them to adapt to newly introduced systems and methods effortlessly.
